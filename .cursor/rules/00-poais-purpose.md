# POAIS Purpose

- **POAIS** is a Product Operating AI System: a structured, markdown-first operating system for product work.
- **Human-led orchestration** — humans decide; AI assists with synthesis and drafting.
- **AI assists** with summarization, extracting decisions, suggesting edits, and composing updates.
- **Inputs are messy** (chat, email, meetings, docs); **outputs are structured** (CONTEXT, PLAN, DECISIONS, STATUS, etc.).
- **POAIS does not replace execution tools** (e.g., GitLab, Jira). It complements them as the alignment and intent layer.
