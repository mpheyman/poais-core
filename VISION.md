# Vision

Product work is fragmented: intent lives in chat, email, and meetings; execution lives elsewhere. Decisions get lost and stakeholders get surprised.

POAIS provides a structured alignment layer that keeps the core problem visible, tracks what is being built, preserves decisions and tradeoffs, surfaces risks early, and makes communication fast and consistent. Humans orchestrate; AI assists with synthesis and drafting. Inputs stay messy; outputs become structured.

poais-core is the distribution of that system — vendored into product repos via subtree, built for [Cursor](https://cursor.com) and using Cursor commands, rules, skills, and subagents as the primary interface.
