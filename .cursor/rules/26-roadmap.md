# ROADMAP

- **Purpose** — ROADMAP is the stakeholder-facing view of key delivery dates and narrative (current quarter, next, themes). It is **fed by** PLAN, EXECUTION, and DECISIONS; the agent and PM keep it in sync.

- **Required structure:**
  - **Milestones** — Key delivery/change dates (feature or phase complete and deployed). Required section. Use a table or consistent list format: Date (YYYY-MM-DD) | Milestone name | Status | Notes. Status uses the same taxonomy as 25-dates-and-deadlines.md: Confirmed | Target | Requested | At risk.
  - **Current Quarter** — What’s in focus this quarter (bullets or short narrative).
  - **Next** — What follows.
  - **Themes** — High-level themes.

- **Feeding** — When proposing or updating ROADMAP, derive milestones from PLAN (phasing, timeline), EXECUTION (what’s shipping when), and DECISIONS (date commitments/changes). Agent may propose new or updated milestone rows; PM approves.

- **Use** — ROADMAP is intended to be referenced or pasted into status emails and stakeholder updates for visibility and alignment. Commands that compose status or alignment should use ROADMAP when present.
